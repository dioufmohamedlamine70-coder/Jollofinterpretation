Who We Are

Young Certified Professionals,
Rooted in Community

Jollof Interpretation Services is an independent contractor interpretation service based in New York City. We are a group of young certified court and medical interpreters who understand firsthand the challenges facing African immigrants in the United States.

We speak your language ‚Äî literally and culturally. Our team provides French, Wolof, and Pulaar interpretation for West African immigrants navigating American legal, medical, academic, and social systems. We serve primarily New York City and all 50 states.
